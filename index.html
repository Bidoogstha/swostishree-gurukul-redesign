A Legacy of Learning

Grounded in Tradition, Inspired by the Future

A world-class education must be anchored in strong values. The Swostishree curriculum integrates the global IB framework with the rich cultural heritage of Nepal — from Saraswoti Puja to Indra Jatra, tradition lives alongside inquiry.

  • Inquiry-based, constructivist learning — students question and investigate, not memorize.
  • Multidisciplinary, balanced curriculum with knowledge-based continuous assessment.
  • Experiential and outdoor learning — including an indoor heated swimming pool.
  • Digital education and e-learning woven into everyday classes.
